# Tariff Impact Analysis - Samsung Electronics Vietnam

## Executive Summary

**Date**: May 17, 2025  
**Company**: Samsung Electronics Vietnam  
**Tariff Rate**: 46%  
**Revenue at Risk**: $21 billion (35% of total revenue)

## Key Findings

### Financial Impact
- **Total Impact**: -$9.87 billion
- **Critical Risk Areas**: 3
- **Immediate Cash Flow Impact**: -$3.15 billion
- **Profit Margin Compression**: -$2.52 billion

### Risk Assessment
1. **Financial** (Severity: 9/10)
   - Severe cash flow reduction
   - Significant margin compression
   
2. **Market** (Severity: 7/10)
   - Potential 20% US market share loss
   - Brand perception challenges
   
3. **Operational** (Severity: 7/10)
   - Supply chain disruption
   - Production reallocation needs

4. **Strategic** (Severity: 8/10)
   - Major strategic pivot required
   - Long-term repositioning needed
